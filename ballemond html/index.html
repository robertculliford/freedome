Welcome to

FREEDOME:
inside Bellamont

A design project in the making.

You will experience Kidd’s adventure through her eyes. Live Bellamont’s tensions and excitements as you let yourself go to absolute freedom.

Your journey through Bellamont won’t always be easy and you will be confronted with challenges. Play to get through and find out what will happen next.

The world counts five portals as entrances to Bellamont. They are hidden in Milan, London, Shanghai, New York and Melbourne.Only those who find them can play.

Part of your exploration of Bellamont is based in real life. This means that the experience will be dotted with events, meet the other players and enjoy Bellamont at its fullest.

In Bellamont, community and sense of belonging are key. Five different playing cities mean an international group of players. As the game develops you will need their help… communication is essential!

Dystopian Bellamont is built as a metaphor of social media. In both worlds it is easy to get trapped in vicious cycles of anonymity,exclusion from physical social life and selective representation of identity.
